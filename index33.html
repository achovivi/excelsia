Internship/Work Placement

Experience in the workplace will allow you to integrate theory with practice and inform your further study and approach to learning. You can undertake an internship or work experience in Australia, in your home country, or in a completely new destination. You must register the internship via CareerHub to be reviewed by the Work Integrated Learning team (internships).

Internships or work experience are offered through the following options

  • 6-month Internships
  • 12-week Work Experience
  • 9-month Internship
